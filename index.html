Hangman Game Project

Click Here to play my assignment 2 version!

Click Here to play my assignment 3 version!

Players are presented with a random word (fruit), and they must guess the letters to reveal the hidden word before making too many incorrect guesses.

Features:

  • Randomly selects a word from a predefined list of fruit names.
  • Displays placeholders for each letter in the word to be guessed.
  • Allows players to click on letter buttons to make guesses.
  • Provides visual feedback on the hangman image based on incorrect guesses.
  • Tracks the number of games won and lost.
  • Offers the option to play again after winning or losing a game.
